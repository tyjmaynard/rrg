Gorge(ous) of Kentucky: Sky Bridge

University of Kentucky Geography

At roughly 85ft long, about 18ft wide, and with a depth of roughly 50ft, Sky Bridge is a natural bridge located near Pine Ridge of the Red River Gorge in Slade, Kentucky. A popular attraction, Sky Bridge boasts a scenic view of the forestry and nearby cliffs and arches. It lies along the Sky Bridge Loop Trail, with a total distance of 0.8-miles.

Sky Bridge is easily accessible from the Sky Bridge Parking Area. From there you traverse past the blacktop loop and embark on a short trek, greeting the adventurer to a breathtaking view of forestry and nearby cliffs and arches atop Sky Bridge.
